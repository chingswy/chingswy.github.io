\begin{rSection}{Project Highlights}

    {\bf Multimodal Generative Models.} Advancing generative models requires a coherent pipeline across data, training, and evaluation. I have built end-to-end pipelines that cover billion-scale motion-text data curation and cleaning, flow-matching training recipes, and evaluation protocols for generation quality and text alignment. As the main driver of \href{https://github.com/Tencent-Hunyuan/HY-Motion-1.0}{HY-Motion 1.0}, I drove it from research to product; it has been open-sourced with 2.5K+ GitHub stars and deployed in Tencent's Hunyuan 3D Studio and Honor of Kings: World, powering end-to-end 3D creation pipelines and AI NPC gameplay.

    {\bf Agentic Systems with Reasoning, Action, and Perception.} Evaluating and improving generative models calls for judgment that goes beyond scalar metrics. This motivates an agent that decides what to inspect, invokes the right tools to perceive it, and reasons over the evidence to produce grounded, structured verdicts. I built such a perceive-reason-act system and applied it to text-to-motion evaluation and to 3D asset quality assessment, where the agent chooses viewpoints and details to examine, renders and inspects the target accordingly, and returns per-dimension diagnostics with localized evidence for each verdict. I am now working on adding closed-loop feedback to the originally open-loop generation pipeline, where the agent's critiques are fed back to guide iterative refinement and repair at inference time.

    {\bf Real-World Reconstruction from Observations.} Downstream generative and agentic models rely on priors grounded in real-world observations, which call for accessible infrastructure to capture and reconstruct them. Toward this end, I built \href{https://github.com/zju3dv/EasyMocap}{EasyMocap} (4.7K+ GitHub stars), which lowers the barrier to human motion capture from sparse multi-camera rigs and in-the-wild internet videos, and \href{https://github.com/zju3dv/LoG}{LoG} (770+ GitHub stars), which supports real-time interactive photorealistic 3D scene reconstruction. Together they push forward 3D reconstruction as accessible tooling and provide reliable data sources for downstream research on generation and world understanding.

\end{rSection}
